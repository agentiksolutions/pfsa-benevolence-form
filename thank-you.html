Application Received

Thank you for submitting your benevolence application. Our team has received your information and will begin the review process.

You will receive a confirmation email at the address you provided.

What Happens Next

Within 2 business days: We'll confirm receipt and check your application for completeness. If anything is missing, we'll reach out.
Within 5 business days: Our review team evaluates your application and supporting documents.
Within 7 business days: The Benevolence Committee makes a decision and you'll be notified of the outcome.
If approved: Funds are disbursed directly to your vendor/provider within 2 business days of approval.
